# Quality Standards

Every knowledge asset should be clear, useful, reusable, and understandable by both humans and AI agents.

## Required Qualities

A strong knowledge asset should have:

- Clear title
- Clear purpose
- Source or context
- Practical use case
- Domain placement
- Review status where applicable

## Avoid

Avoid unclear drafts, duplicated knowledge, unverified claims, missing context, and files without clear ownership or purpose.
