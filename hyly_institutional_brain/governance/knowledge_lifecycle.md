# Knowledge Lifecycle

Knowledge should move through a clear lifecycle.

```text
Capture
↓
Structure
↓
Review
↓
Publish
↓
Maintain
↓
Archive
```

## Capture

Knowledge is identified from existing work, meetings, courses, product understanding, AI research, client insights, or team discussions.

## Structure

Knowledge is placed in the correct domain and formatted as a reusable asset.

## Review

Knowledge is checked for accuracy, clarity, and usefulness.

## Publish

Knowledge is added to the repository and becomes available for S.Academy, humans, or AI agents.

## Maintain

Knowledge should be updated when it becomes outdated or incomplete.

## Archive

Old knowledge should be archived instead of deleted when it may still be useful for historical context.
