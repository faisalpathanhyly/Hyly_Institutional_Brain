## Onboarding Intelligence Ownership

`onboarding_intelligence/` is a repository-level brain for employee onboarding, training, evaluation, and performance refinement.

It is not directly mapped as a S.Academy knowledge domain. It uses S.Academy as the learning delivery platform while maintaining its own onboarding structure and ownership.

### Purpose

The purpose of `onboarding_intelligence/` is to provide a structured system for onboarding new recruits and teams.

This brain supports:

- training plans for new recruits
- product intelligence for onboarding
- evaluation plans and assets
- mindmaps for new recruits
- onboarding checklists
- performance tracking
- goal tracking
- refinement of employee performance gaps

The goal is to create a smoother onboarding experience, evaluate learning and readiness, improve employee performance, and reduce repeated hiring and firing cycles by identifying gaps earlier.

### Primary Owner

KM Team

### Ownership Responsibilities

The owner is responsible for:

- maintaining the `onboarding_intelligence/` folder structure
- keeping onboarding plans updated
- organizing onboarding assets by purpose
- coordinating with managers and department leads
- linking to S.Academy learning material where relevant
- ensuring onboarding assets do not duplicate full S.Academy course content
- maintaining evaluation plans and readiness assets
- updating checklists and mindmaps when onboarding processes change
- maintaining performance tracking and refinement assets


### S.Academy Relationship

`onboarding_intelligence/` may reference S.Academy courses, videos, quizzes, or learning assets.

However, it should not duplicate full S.Academy course content.

For example:

```text
S.Academy stores the learning course.
onboarding_intelligence defines when, why, and how that course is used in onboarding.
```

### Ownership Rule

Any major update to onboarding structure, training plans, evaluation assets, or performance tracking assets should be reviewed by the relevant owner before being treated as active onboarding material.
