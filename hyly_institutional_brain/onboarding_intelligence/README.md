# onboarding_intelligence

## Purpose

`onboarding_intelligence` is the institutional brain for employee onboarding, training, evaluation, and performance refinement.

It exists to provide a structured onboarding system for new recruits and teams. The goal is to help Hyly train employees, evaluate their understanding, refine performance gaps, and track onboarding goals in a consistent way.

This brain does not replace S.Academy.

S.Academy is the learning delivery platform. `onboarding_intelligence` defines the onboarding structure, training plans, evaluation assets, checklists, mindmaps, and performance refinement process that can use S.Academy content.

---

## What This Brain Does

`onboarding_intelligence` organizes the assets needed to support the full onboarding journey.

It includes:

- training plans for new recruits
- product intelligence for onboarding
- evaluation plans and assessment assets
- mindmaps for learning paths
- checklists for onboarding completion
- performance tracking and refinement assets

The goal is to create a seamless onboarding and training experience that helps new recruits learn faster, get evaluated clearly, and improve based on structured feedback.

---

## How It Uses S.Academy

`onboarding_intelligence` is not directly mapped as a S.Academy knowledge domain.

Instead, it uses S.Academy as the learning platform.

For example:

- S.Academy may host the learning course.
- `onboarding_intelligence` defines when and why the recruit should complete that course.
- S.Academy may deliver quizzes or learning modules.
- `onboarding_intelligence` defines the evaluation plan and readiness criteria.
- S.Academy may contain product learning assets.
- `onboarding_intelligence` connects those assets to role-based onboarding plans.

## Folder Definitions

### training_plans/

Stores role-based or cohort-based training plans for new recruits.

### product_intelligence/

Stores product-related onboarding knowledge assets, product learning paths, video references, and links to relevant S.Academy courses.

### evaluation_assets/

Stores evaluation plans, scorecards, readiness checks, and assessment assets used to evaluate learning and performance.

### mindmaps/

Stores mindmaps or structured learning maps for new recruits.

### checklists/

Stores onboarding checklists for recruits, managers, trainers, and reviewers.

### performance_tracking/

Stores assets used to track onboarding progress, goals, gaps, and refinement plans.

## Future Scope

The future scope of `onboarding_intelligence` includes:

- role-based onboarding journeys
- cohort-based onboarding plans
- product-specific training paths
- evaluation scorecards for new recruits
- performance gap tracking
- goal tracking for onboarding
- refinement plans based on evaluation results
- manager-facing onboarding dashboards
- training effectiveness reviews

The long-term goal is to reduce unclear onboarding, improve employee readiness, track real performance, and reduce the repeated hiring and firing cycle by identifying gaps earlier and supporting employees with structured refinement.
