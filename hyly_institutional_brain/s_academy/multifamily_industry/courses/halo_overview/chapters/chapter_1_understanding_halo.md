# Chapter 1: Understanding Halo

## **1.1 What is Halo?**
Halo is a unified reporting and marketing intelligence platform built for multifamily teams. It helps organizations bring together marketing, operational, and leasing data into one connected system for faster and more confident decision-making.
Instead of managing disconnected dashboards and manually comparing reports across systems, Halo creates a centralized source of truth that teams can trust.
#### 1.1.1 What Halo Helps Teams Do
- Connect data across systems
- Improve reporting accuracy
- Understand marketing performance
- Track leasing journeys
- Reduce manual reporting work
- Improve operational visibility

A. It centralizes reporting and performance visibility

## 1.2 Why Halo Exists
Many teams spend hours pulling reports from different systems and manually comparing numbers that often do not match.
Halo was built to solve:
- disconnected reporting systems
- inaccurate marketing attribution
- inconsistent dashboards
- manual reporting processes
- operational inefficiencies
