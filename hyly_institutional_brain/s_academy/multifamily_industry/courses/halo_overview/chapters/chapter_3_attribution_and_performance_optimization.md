# Chapter 3: Attribution & Performance Optimization

## **3.1 **Attribution Models & CrSTAL Framework
Halo supports:
- First-touch attribution
- Last-touch attribution
- Multi-touch attribution
The CrSTAL framework tracks prospects across:
Created → Scheduled → Toured → Applied → Leased

**Knowledge Check:**
**Question:** A prospect clicks an ad, returns through another channel, tours, and later signs a lease. Which Halo capability helps track this journey?
A. Attribution and CrSTAL lifecycle tracking
B. Manual spreadsheet notes for each contact
C. One-time campaign reporting without context
D. Resident-only communication history tracking
**Correct Answer:** A — Attribution and CrSTAL lifecycle tracking are correct because they help connect touchpoints across the prospect journey.
## **3.2 **Spend Manager & Conversion Insights
Spend Manager helps teams understand:
- cost per click
- cost per lead
- cost per tour
- cost per lease
- campaign ROI
#### Metrics Tracked
| Metric | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Cost Per Lead | Lead generation efficiency |
| Cost Per Tour | Tour acquisition performance |
| Cost Per Lease | Leasing ROI |
| Conversion Rates | Funnel performance |
| Journey Velocity | Speed through leasing funnel |
[https://res.cloudinary.com/dtlycu2wl/video/upload/v1781525781/Halo_Spend_Manager_rptobs.mp4](https://res.cloudinary.com/dtlycu2wl/video/upload/v1781525781/Halo_Spend_Manager_rptobs.mp4)
#### Key Takeaways
- Teams can optimize advertising spend
- ROI visibility supports budgeting decisions
- Conversion analysis improves marketing efficiency
**Knowledge Check:**
**Question:** Marketing spend is increasing, but the team cannot see which channels are driving leases. Which Halo feature helps?
A. Spend Manager showing ROI and conversion insights
B. Static budget summaries without lease outcomes
C. Manual campaign notes from individual teams
D. Property calendars showing resident events
**Correct Answer:** A — Spend Manager is correct because it connects marketing spend to performance, ROI, and leasing outcomes.
