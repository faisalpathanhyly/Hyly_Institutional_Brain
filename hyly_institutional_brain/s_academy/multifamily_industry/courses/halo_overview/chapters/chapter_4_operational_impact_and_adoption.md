**Knowledge Check:**
**Question:** A leadership team wants to understand Halo’s real operational value. Which outcome best reflects Halo’s impact?
A. Faster decisions through clearer reporting visibility
B. Fewer insights because data stays disconnected
C. More manual work across separate platforms
D. Less alignment between marketing and operations
**Correct Answer:** A — Faster decisions through clearer reporting visibility is correct because Halo helps teams act on connected and reliable performance data.

- Schedule recurring reporting workflows
- Review attribution data regularly
- Use centralized reporting for decision-making
- Align operational and marketing reporting
