# Chapter 3: Description — How Do I Tell AI What I Need?

This chapter introduces the second D: **Description**. Description is not just “prompt writing.” It is the skill of expressing intent so clearly that AI, a product system, or another person can execute correctly.

## 3.1 What Description Means

### Watch: The Art of Description

**Description = Telling AI exactly what you need, how to think, and how to behave — so it does not have to guess.**

### Knowledge Check

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Why does AI often produce poor output from vague prompts? | Because AI fills gaps by guessing, and unclear instructions usually lead to unclear or unusable output. |

## 3.2 Product, Process, and Performance

https://res.cloudinary.com/dtlycu2wl/image/upload/v1776938716/ch3_description_dimensions_bcia6s.png

| Dimension | What It Controls | Think of It As | Example Phrases |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Product** | What you want | The order form | “200-word email, bullet format, professional tone, for property managers” |
| **Process** | How AI should think | The method | “Think step by step,” “consider both sides,” “start with the most important point” |
| **Performance** | How AI should behave | The role | “Act as a critical editor,” “be a supportive brainstormer,” “respond as an expert” |

## 3.3 Bad vs. Good Prompts

### Example 1: Writing an Email

|  | Bad Prompt | Good Prompt |
|---|---|---|
| What was written | “Write an email to a prospect” | “Write a 3-sentence follow-up email to a multifamily property manager who attended our Halo demo. Tone: warm and professional. Goal: book a 30-min next call.” |
| Result | You spend 20 minutes editing | You spend 2 minutes tweaking |

### Example 2: Building a Report

|  | Bad Prompt | Good Prompt |
|---|---|---|
| What was written | “Make a report on occupancy trends” | “Summarize occupancy trends for Q1 2025 for a Sunbelt market portfolio. Use bullet points. Audience: VP of Operations. Include 3 key takeaways and one risk flag.” |
| Result | A long wall of text with no structure | A sharp, structured summary the VP can scan in 60 seconds |

### Knowledge Check

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| What makes a good prompt stronger than a bad prompt? | It gives AI clear context, format, audience, tone, goal, thinking direction, and constraints. |

## 3.4 Description Checklist and Throne Connection

- Did I specify the **format**?
- Did I specify the **audience**?
- Did I specify the **tone**?
- Did I tell AI **how to think**?
- Did I set a **role**?
- Did I include **constraints**?
