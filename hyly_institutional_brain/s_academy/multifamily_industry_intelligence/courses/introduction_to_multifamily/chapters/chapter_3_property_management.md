## 3.2 How is the Property Management Team structured?

Property management teams bring together specialists across operations to ensure properties are managed consistently and professionally.

Teams are typically organized into two levels:

- **Corporate teams** — focused on strategy, reporting, marketing, and portfolio oversight
- **Onsite teams** — focused on leasing, resident experience, and daily operations

Property Management Companies (PMCs) are Hyly.AI’s primary clients.

PMCs use Hyly.AI’s tools to support:

- Leasing and marketing
- Prospect and resident communication
- Reporting and operational visibility
- Workflow automation and efficiency

As the industry evolves, many PMCs are moving toward **centralized operating models**, with key functions like leasing, renewals, accounting, and resident services increasingly supported by AI-powered tools.
